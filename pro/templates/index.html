FSI Features

Fragile State Index of any place is depend on various features of the respective country , that are.

Security Aparatus

The Security Apparatus indicator considers the security threats to a state, such as bombings, attacks and battle-related deaths, rebel movements, mutinies, coups, or terrorism.

Learn More

Economic Decline and Property

It define the current economical state of a country and value of it's currency.

Learn More

state legitimacy

State legitimacy is a key aspect of state-society relations.

Learn More

Human Rights and Rule of Law

it represents the state laws and human rights granted by it and their implementation.

Learn More

Refugees and Internally Displaced Persons.

A refugee is a person who has left the country of their citizenship because they fear persecution over their race, religion, nationality, beliefs or membership of a particular social group..

Learn More

External Intervention

External intervention can be either neutral (as it is in all of the above examples), or partisan. .
